Principles

Four rules the system never breaks

01
Nothing decorative

Every element earns its place. If it doesn't carry meaning, it doesn't exist. Form follows function — and becomes beautiful because of it.

02
Structure is visible

The grid, the hierarchy, the thinking — all readable. An architectural model shows its structure. So does this portfolio.

03
Tension, not decoration

Steel and signal red. Serif weight against condensed precision. The contrast is intentional — it reflects how the work actually feels.

04
Move only when it matters

Stillness is confidence. Animation appears only to orient, reveal, or confirm — never to impress.
